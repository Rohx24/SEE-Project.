RV University

This was a project I started back in November 2023 when I first learned HTML. I worked on this with my friend as a fun to do project.

The website helps student's who are looking for an admission into a college.The website is user-friendly and aimed to provide customers with valuable and useful data.

Upon opening the website you are greeted with the college photo and the courses it has to offer.

Customers can select from a drop-down list about what they are looking for.They can also apply for the college's own entrance exam.

The customer is then re-directed to the webpage they have selected.

My team worked on this for 2 weeks.
