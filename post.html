Car Quest

This was a project I started back in December 2023 when I first learned HTML. I worked on this with my friend as a first year mini project.

The website helps consumer's who were in the market to buy a car.The website is user-friendly and aimed to provide customers with valuable and useful data.

Upon logging into the website you would be requested to enter your budget and what category of car you are looking for.

Customers are then re-directed to a new webpage showcasing the car's that would fit their budget and a brief description about the car.

If the customer like's the car shown to them, They are then re-directed to the manufacturers webpage where they can book a test drive for the car.

My team worked on this for around a month before selling it to a parent company.
